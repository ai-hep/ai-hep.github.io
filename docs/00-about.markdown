Welcom to our **AI+HEP in East Asia** (pronounced as ``I Happier``) community! We are a self-organized group of high energy physicists (mostly in the East Asia region) enthusiastic about artificial intelligence and its potentially profound revolution in natural sciences. 

In our research, we develop novel AI methods tailored to the special needs of HEP and use them to solve myriad challenges in particle theory, particle experiments, astrophysics, cosmology, and more. We are equally interested in using tools developed in the theoretical physics context and our knowledge about complex systems to understand neural networks and design better next-generation AI systems.     

We organize many academic activities throughout the year, including an annual weeklong in-person workshop, monthly remote seminars, regular journal clubs, as well as developing a set of curriculum to train our students interested in pursuing this field. Through open discussions and active idea exchanges, we hope to gradually form a set of new, well-defined, and meaningful scientific questions in AI+HEP and chart out a path for the development of our field in the coming decades.
